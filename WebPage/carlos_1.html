RAN Dimensioning

This widget calculates the Material forecast and the Space Requirements during the different phases of the initiative based on a general Deployment Plan.

In order to use this widget, perform the following steps:

  • Introduce the characteristics of the Deployment Plan and the Equipment Dimensions, according to the given units.
  • Click on the "Calculate" button to generate the Warehouse Storage Space Requirement.
  • The Warehouse Storage Space Requirement can be found in the Results section.

Basics for this widget are addressed on the Logistics & Warehousing Module.
